Magazine Proposal

01: Audience

Demography: Age, Gender, Ethnicity, geography, and income

Mostly young adults of any ethnicity and incomes starting to focus on creating their own lifestyles after college.


02: Form - Types of messages. imagery, and visual approach

Use different types of minimalist imagery and illustrations to get the point across that an eco-friendly lifestyle can be very simple to accomplish.


03: Definition of your magazine

This magazine will focus on developing a positive mindset on our readers about environmentalism and changing their behaviors to fit that agenda. It will include a list of articles that will demonstrate how easy it is to simply replace some specific habits with more eco-friendly actions that are meant for the greater good.


04: Objectives

The main objective is to present alternative ways of living which can help build new habits that will later be passed on to generations which will slowly help the planet little by little.
