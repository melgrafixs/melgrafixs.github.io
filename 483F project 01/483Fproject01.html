Design Concept

Although we may not be as conscious of it happening, the world in which we live in and the high-technology developed over the years are gradually interwining into one. Nowadays, more and more people turn to the web for not just any kind of information but also to promote their own selves whether it be for work, art exposure, dating life, coaching others, etc. Interestingly, this project allowed me to look back at how I present myself to this new world of technology through social media. I found that often times I portray myself as a soft, happy, and sweet persona through the colors I choose to be associated with and the style I tend to lean towards. As you can tell, soft pastel colors combined with bright cheerful colors are the type of design and persona I like to illustrate. Knowing this, I followed the same principles across the other elements I needed to design such as posters, an interface layout, and coffee cups.
